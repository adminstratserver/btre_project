As a SaaS service provider, our system automates trade orders directly into our client's trading plaform. It is no different from outsourcing the monitoring and trade executions based on your choice of a trading algorithm.

    This is a simple 3 steps process as follows:


    1. Client select the trading algorithm file based on his risk-rewards appetite from the large range of algorithm files (OptionNalytics Algo-Library).
    2. A dedicated server (OptionNalytics Stratserver) is setup which is loaded with the algo-file, and the server is connected to the client's IB trading PC via internet. Stratserver is given authorisation by client's PC to send trade orders into IB trading platform.
    3. When the rule-based algo triggers a trade order and execution, the trade order is sent from Stratserver to the client's trading PC. An alert notification message is also sent to the clients HP.
