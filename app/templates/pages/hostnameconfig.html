Hostname Configuration SetUp

Your trading PC (IB Gateway PC) may be connected to internet via a dynamic IP address that is assigned by your ISP.
To ensure the trade orders reaches your trading PC, its important to associate your IP address with a Hostname.
You may do so using services from Dynamic DNS (DDNS) Host Providers.

The following is list of recommended DDNS host providers:

      NO-IP
      Oracle Dynamic DNS
      SecurePoint
      DYNDNS Service

Finally, the client PC is usually sitting behind a domestic router having its its internal router address ie. 192.168.XXX.XXX. The last step is to setup the router to port forward all incoming messages to via port 7496 to the trading PC.
